- Utah Food Bank, Food Drive -
On Saturday, November 19th the Ivins 4th Ward primary-age boys will be conducting a Food Drive
for Utah Food Bank. If you wish to participate, we would request you have any non-perishable
food items bagged and placed on your porch or by your driveway. The Primary Boys will start

for contributing to the Utah Food Bank for those in need.

Explore a life-size replica of the Tabernacle from the Old Testament now thru December 13.

Ticket information for devotionals and tabernacle tours is available at Tabernacle2022.com. 
Tours take about 45-60 minutes. To volunteer contact Mark Crump, 435-313-1655 & mcrump@infowest.com.
